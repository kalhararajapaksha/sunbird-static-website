ABOUT SUNBIRD LANKA TOURS & TRAVELS

Make your travel expectations come true with our best services.

Sunbird Lanka Tours & Travels (Pvt) Ltd. is a Tour Agency approved by the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority as an ‘Inbound’ Tour Operator and located in close proximity to the City of Colombo in the Western Province of Sri Lanka. Our Tour Company provides travelers with professional, value added and exciting travel services, with the capacity of catering to a full range of travel solutions, by giving the best value for their money.

Our aim is to make your holiday the most memorable and interesting, and for that, we work and grow together as a team. We are geared to meet all the challenges we encounter in the field of competition, and make maximum endeavor to preserve the quality of our services. Our fleet of Brand New Vehicles gives us an added advantage and its real meaning to our Excellent Services. Passengers traveling by our vehicles are fully insured, and served by expert Chauffeurs/ Guides.
